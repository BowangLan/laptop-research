INFO 340 Project Proposal: Laptop Finder

Problem Description

Overview

Technology such as laptops are an expensive investment so how can consumers make the best decision on a laptop purchase that fits their needs.

Information Technology Problem

It can be difficult to find a laptop that fits your exact needs. For example, you might be able to find a laptop that matches some of your preferences, like brand and price range, but that doesnâ€™t fit your other preferences.

Existing Attempt

LaptopMedia - a previos attempt for this problem that allows user to filter out laptops based on their preferences.

Proposed Application

Users

The people who use our app would be anybody who is looking to find a new laptop. This might include college students, gamers, or anybody who uses a laptop for work.

Data

The user will be viewing information about different laptops such as the brand, model, and price. We will be drawing data from the source cited below.

Kumar, S. (2022, April 3). Laptop specs and latest price. Kaggle. Retrieved from https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/kuchhbhi/latest-laptop-price-list

Interactive Features

  • Quiz: Provide a short quiz to give recommendations to users who might not initially know what kind of laptop they want.
  • Filtering: Allow users to check off different preferences they have, like the minimum amount of storage they need their laptop to have, to help them narrow down search results.
  • Compare tool: Allow users to compare similar laptops.

App Solution

Our app would be a solution because it would initially provide laptop recommendations based off of the quiz the user fills out. From there, the user is able to adjust different filters to easily find a laptop that suits their needs. Once the user has found a few laptops that meet their needs, they can then use our compare tool to compare these options and make their final selection. In the end, our app will allow users to quickly and easily find a laptop with their preferred specs.
